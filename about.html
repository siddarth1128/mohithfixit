About FixItNow

Revolutionizing home services through technology - connecting homeowners with trusted professionals

Project Overview

FixItNow - All Services at One Place

A comprehensive web platform transforming the home services industry

FixItNow is an innovative web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between homeowners and local home service professionals. Our mission is to simplify the process of finding, booking, and managing home services while empowering skilled professionals to grow their businesses.

The platform connects users with verified local service providers including plumbers, electricians, appliance technicians, carpenters, and more based on real-time location and availability. Users can easily view service provider profiles, book jobs, and track service status through an intuitive interface.

For service providers, FixItNow offers powerful tools to register, manage job requests, update availability, and communicate effectively with clients. Our advanced features include secure authentication, role-based access control, real-time notifications, and asynchronous processing using message queues for seamless booking confirmations and job updates.

The Challenge

Problem Statement

Addressing the critical gaps in the home services industry

Time-Consuming Process

Traditional methods of finding reliable home service professionals involve word-of-mouth referrals, unverified online listings, or outdated contact directories, causing significant delays in getting services.

Lack of Reliability

Homeowners often face unreliable service providers, unavailability issues, and substandard service quality due to the absence of proper verification and rating systems.

Limited Opportunities for Providers

Local service professionals struggle with limited digital visibility, relying on manual bookings and local contacts, which restricts their customer base and earning potential.

Inefficient Management

Both customers and service providers lack centralized tools for managing bookings, availability, job status tracking, and effective communication, leading to disjointed experiences.
